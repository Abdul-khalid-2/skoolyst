Project: Skoolyst
Developer: Abdul Khalid
Start Date: May 2025
What, Why, and How?
Introduction

I wanted to build a real project that could solve a real problem in our society. I started thinking deeply about the major issues people face in their 
daily lives. I also discussed these issues with friends and colleagues to understand real-world problems.


I received feedback about many serious issues in our society, such as:

Health problems
Traffic issues
Environmental pollution
Global warming
Weak education systems
Business-related challenges
Social and festival-related issues
There are many problems, and I realized that it would not be possible for me to solve all of them alone. So I started thinking differently.
The Root Cause

I asked myself:

The world is becoming advanced day by day, so why are we still facing these challenges?
After research and reflection, I realized that one of the biggest root causes is lack of knowledge, awareness, guidance, and proper training among people.
But how can we improve awareness and guidance at a large scale?
The answer was clear: Schools.
Schools are the foundation of society. They are responsible for guiding and training children from an early age. If children receive proper education, 
awareness, and skill development, they grow up to become responsible and capable citizens.
The Real Problem I Observed
One day, I was in the office when our office assistant looked very stressed. I asked him what happened. He told me that his children were being dropped 
from their school. He said:




He was paying high fees.
He was not financially strong.
The school suddenly decided to remove his children.
He did not know which school would be better for his kids.
He had no proper information about other schools.
He asked me if I could suggest a good school.
I wanted to help, but I realized something important:

I did not have complete information about schools either — such as:



School type
Curriculum
Fee structure
Facilities
Faculty
Environment
Class levels
This is not just one person’s problem. It is a problem faced by the majority of parents.
The Core Issue


Many parents:

Do not have complete information about schools.
Choose schools without understanding their requirements.
Later face problems when expectations do not match reality.


Many schools:

Admit students without clearly communicating policies.
Do not provide transparent information.
Later remove students due to mismatched expectations.
This creates stress for:

Parents
Students
Schools

And it negatively impacts children’s confidence and education.

The Solution: Skoolys

After observing and researching this issue, I decided to create a platform called Skoolyst.


Vision of Skoolyst

Skoolyst is designed to:

Help parents find and compare schools easily.
Provide complete and transparent school information.
Allow schools to showcase their details properly.
Reduce misunderstandings between parents and schools.
Support better decision-making for children's education.
When children receive the right education from the right institution, they grow into skilled, aware, and responsible individuals. 
Over time, this can help improve many other societal problems as well.

Conclusion

Skoolyst is not just a school listing platform.

It is a step toward improving education transparency and helping parents make informed decisions for their children’s future.
By strengthening education from childhood, we can indirectly contribute to solving many larger societal challenges.
